Must Read Queer Science Fiction

Afternoon at McBurgers

By: Ana Galvañ

Find "Afternoon at McBurgers" on WorldCat.org

One of the strangest, yet most compelling graphic novels best read while society teeters on the edge of transcendent change due to unchecked capitalism. So, like right now. Taking place in a high-school-tinged, not-McDonalds where a few lucky guests get a glimpse of something not-meant-to-be-seen, a couple of girlfriends make a practical decision.
