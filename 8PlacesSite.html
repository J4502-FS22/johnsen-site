8 Places to be When You’re Under 21 in Kansas City

It can be hard being under 21 in a city in the Midwest. Here's some places I loved growing up in KC.

Prospero's Books

1800 W 39th St
Kansas City, MO
64111
(816) 531-9673

Visit Prospero's website

This quaint three-story bookstore is a beautiful backdrop for an afternoon with friends, a date, or just some alone time. Make sure you stay for the local live music upstairs!       

The Nelson Atkins

44525 Oak St
Kansas City, MO
64111
(816) 751-1278

Visit The Nelson Atkins' website

Oh you know, just the world class museum in the heart of the Art District. I've been more times than I can count, and I still haven't seen everything. Did I mention it's free?      

The Plaza

9033 Manchester Road
Kansas City, MO
64112
(816) 753-0100

Visit The Plaza's website

One part place to spend money, one part place to walk around: a midwesterner's two favorite things! I specifically recommend The Big Cheddar.        

Roanoke Park

3601 Roanoke Road
Kansas City, MO
64111
(816) 513-7500

Visit Roanoke Park's website

This is just a quite park in a neighborhood not far from the state line. The best part is that there's playgrounds made much more the size for adults/teens. A fun place to spend a nice day.       

Crown Center Ice Skating

2425 Grand Blvd
Kansas City, MO
64108
(816) 274-8411

Visit Crown Center Ice Skating's website

This is dependant on the time of year of course, but for a reasonable price you can enjoy one of the few benefits to it being so damn cold in KC: ice!  Once you're done you can enjoy the Christmas village set up around the place too. Perfect for pictures.      

Grinter’s Sunflower farm

24154 Stillwell Rd
Lawrence, KS
66044

Visit Grinter’s Sunflower Farm's website

If it's not time for iceskating, it's probably time for sunflowers! Just a short (40 min) drive from downtown, you can marvel at sunflower fields as far the eye can see. There's no better place to see it than Kansas. While you're there, stop by Lawrence!           

The Kansas City Zoo

6800 Zoo Dr
Kansas City, MO
64132
(816) 595-1234

Visit The Kansas City Zoo's website

Of the few reasons to visit Swope Park... well this is the only reason to visit Swope Park (kidding!). A beautiful conservation focused zoo. Fun fact: if there's a heat advisory, discount tickets             

The City Market

20 E. 5th Street
Kansas City, MO
64106
(816) 842-1271

Visit The City Market's website

Remember what I said about walking around and spending money? There's plenty of it! Visit the farmers' market on the weekends, and stop by the asian market while you're there.
